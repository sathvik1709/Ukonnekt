VISION STATEMENT

  • To provide an Interface between the student and the professor

  • Get Information related to the University and the Courses

  • Avoid Missing a deadline

    • 7 Simple Steps to STAY CONNECTED TO YOUR UNIVERSITY:

    1 Log in with your NetID and Password

    2 You can view the Time-Table for Today as well as upcoming two days

    3 Swipe in from the side to access the Action Drawer which shows the List of Courses taken by you

    4 Click on Course to access the course details

    5 Swipe on the swipe tabs to access Course Details like 'Announcements', 'Assignments', 'Grades', 'Feed back' and 'Schedule'

    6 Click on Events to access the events and News provided by UTA ShortHorn

    7 You can Log Out any time by pressing on the Log Out button in the Action Overflow menu
